About MetaVirt

Bangladesh's trusted partner for software-defined infrastructure and enterprise cloud solutions.

Who We Are

MetaVirt Information Technologies Private Limited is a Bangladesh-based enterprise infrastructure software company. We specialize in helping organizations modernize their IT infrastructure with private cloud, virtualization, storage, backup, and container platforms.

Unlike traditional vendors who lock customers into expensive proprietary hardware, MetaVirt delivers software-only solutions that run on your choice of commodity servers. This approach delivers enterprise-grade capabilities at significantly lower total cost of ownership.

Our team combines deep technical expertise with local market understanding to design, deploy, and support infrastructure solutions that meet the unique requirements of Bangladeshi enterprises.
